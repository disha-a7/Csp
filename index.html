About Us

We started as a team of four, with the base idea of the project given by Uzair. The focus was to figure out solutions for existing problems rather than starting a new one every time we had a group presentation. Our theme is from our last SIP project on how to empower awareness in areas affected by poverty in Hyderabad. The aim is to track real-time data using technology and programming to create a solution-focused website.

Divakshi is responsible for the technical development of the website.
Disha manages the database system.
Juveria conducts research on the poverty issues we address.

Mission

This website promotes real-time tracking of areas in Hyderabad affected by poverty. Our databases will help identify which areas lack resources and determine the approximate quantities required. By leveraging this data, we can direct resources efficiently.
